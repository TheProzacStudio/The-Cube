# The Cube Project Document #

## The Cube ##
You start at the most upper level of The Cube. The main goal is to get to the ground floor and leave complex. The difficulty is not based around the tasks but around keeping you and your friends sane.

## Main Location [The Cube] ##
Every level of The Cube has the same amount of Trial Chambers, only one of those has a vault leading further down. If your current room is located next to The Threshold there will be an exit sign above those doors.

## Trial Chambers [Rooms] ##
1. __The Prozac Trials__, there is a table located in the center of the room with pills. The amount of pills reflects the number of players. Each player takes a pill, then the lights above every door switch on.

# Definitions #

| Name | Explanation |
|--|--|
| Pills | 7 kinds of pills: Prozac, Candy, Rage, Panic, Heal, Synaesthesia, Arachnofobia |
| Trial Chamber | Rooms with traps, tasks or straight up torture devices |
| The Threshold | A room with a vault in the middle leading down to the next level |

## Itens Definitions ##

| Item | Explanation |
|--|--|
| Prozac Pill | You don't loose sanity for some time |
| Candy Pill | You gain sanity |
| Rage Pill | You loose your mind and start attacking your friends |
| Panic Pill | You run uncontrollably while screaming, your friends lose sanity while you panic |
| Heal Pill | One of your limbs regrow, if you have all your limbs attached you gain sanity |
| Synaesthesia Pill | Your vision goes black, unless there is sound, your vision is your hearing now |
| Arachnofobia Pill | Your friends randomly turn into spiders, if you look at them you scream and loose sanity |

## Mechanics ##
| Mechanic | Explanation |
|--|--|
| Scream | Some events when you loose sanity cause you to scream, your scream irritates your friends causing them to loose sanity too|
